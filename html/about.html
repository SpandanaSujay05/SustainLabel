Crafting Sustainability with Love & Purpose

About SustainLabel

Imagine a world where beautiful, everyday objects not only elevate your life but also uplift the lives of others. This is the essence of SustainLabel, a company that blends creativity with social responsibility.

We at SustainLabel aim to build a conscious consumer ecosystem. We bring to you hand-crafted products made from eco-friendly raw materials and discarded materials. Our current production line collaborates with organizations working towards an inclusive world, helping create a continuous source of income for those typically excluded from economic activities, like People with Disabilities (PWDs), Self-Help Groups (SHGs), and women from urban/rural poor communities.

Our Mission

At the heart of SustainLabel is a commitment to empowering marginalized communities. We work with skilled individuals who bring unique talent and cultural heritage into each product.

Our Values

  • Inclusivity: Empowering artisans with special needs.
  • Sustainability: Using eco-friendly materials.
  • Craftsmanship: Handcrafted products with deep cultural roots.

SustainLabel blends creativity with social responsibility, crafting sustainable products made by people with special needs, urban poor women, and rural artisans.

A Tapestry of Talent:

Sustainable Threads:

  • Upcycled treasures: Discarded materials get a new lease on life, transformed into bags, notebooks, and home accents that tell a story of conscious consumption.
  • Natural fibers: Cotton, Jute, Linen, and Bamboo form the foundation of our collections, ensuring comfort, durability, and a gentle touch on the environment.
  • Natural, plant-based, and low-impact dyes and finishes: Vibrant colors come alive without compromising the well-being of artisans or the health of the planet.

A Symphony of Design:

SustainLabel is more than just sustainable materials and skilled hands. Their products are infused with creativity, reflecting a deep understanding of modern aesthetics and functionality.

  • From traditional to contemporary: Classic patterns get a fresh twist, while modern designs are imbued with cultural influences, creating a unique fusion of styles.
  • Everyday elegance: Every piece is crafted for daily use, adding a touch of understated beauty to your home and wardrobe.

Let their products be a testament to the transformative power of human potential, a celebration of diversity, and a gentle reminder that small changes can weave a brighter tomorrow.

Our Work

We source eco-friendly materials from local markets, give production work to our collaboration partners, and take the product to the market.

Our work provides a source of employment and income for communities that are typically not included in the economic streams of society. For example, some repetitive tasks like block printing can be done by people on the autism spectrum that our partner organizations work with, and tailoring or stitching work would be taken by mothers, even though they are not directly employed by the partner organizations.
