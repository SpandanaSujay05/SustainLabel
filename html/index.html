Frequently Asked Questions

What materials do you use in making the products?

We use eco-friendly materials available in local markets and discarded fabric material from textile plants and stores. These are all carefully handpicked.

Where are the raw materials sourced from?

We source our products from markets in and around Karnataka, local markets, or textile plants and stores.

How do you choose your collaboration partners?

We collaborate with organizations working towards building an inclusive world, those working with the differently abled, and women's groups.

Can you customise a product?

Yes, we customize products for bulk orders. The size of the order varies based on the request and the availability of raw materials.
